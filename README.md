# Runback

Runback is an AI-powered career preparation platform designed to help students build confidence, improve their resumes, and prepare for internships and full-time opportunities.

The platform gives students practical feedback on how they present their experiences and helps them better understand what employers are looking for.

## Key Features

- AI-powered resume analysis
- Specific resume improvement recommendations
- Personalized mock interviews
- Role-specific interview questions
- Feedback on interview performance
- Career preparation tools built for students
- Guidance for communicating experiences more effectively

## How It Works

1. Create an account or log in.
2. Upload your resume for analysis.
3. Review the feedback and suggested improvements.
4. Select the type of interview you want to practice.
5. Generate personalized interview questions.
6. Complete the mock interview.
7. Review your results and identify areas for improvement.

## Who It Is For

Runback is designed primarily for:

- College students
- Internship applicants
- Recent graduates
- Students with limited professional experience
- Anyone looking to improve their interview confidence

## Mission

Runback is more than a mock interview platform.

Our goal is to help students become more prepared for an evolving workforce by giving them access to personalized career support, actionable feedback, and opportunities to practice communicating their value.
